About Me

I am a public policy researcher with the Takshashila Institution's Geostrategy Programme, and a doctoral scholar at the Manipal Academy of Higher Studies (MAHE).

My work closely tracks the global geopolitical and geoeconomic trends. With a broader focus on China, I study issues at the intersection of the economy, technology and security.

I also teach an Expert Capsule Course on China in the Changing Geo-economic Landscape.
